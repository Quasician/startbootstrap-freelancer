About

There is a clear need for process improvement and greater operational efficiency within Air Force Reserve training. Supervisors struggle to synthesize organized training schedules due to a complex system of data management that requires extensive communication between Microsoft Excel spreadsheets.

EZTrain technology algorithmically identifies and collates relevant training information from large spreadsheets of data, with little input required from the user. By mapping every datapoint to a unique account, the application makes training profiles allowing for more personalized and organized training plans.

Seymour Johnson AFB

We are currently working with senior leadership at Seymour Johnson Air Force Base to make reserve training more efficient and increase communication between Air Force reserve members.

100+ Interviews

From extensive interviews with leaders from all levels of Seymour Johnson Air Force Base, we understand that trainees are often unable to fully utilize their training time due to communication issues, poor accessibility to computers, and the sheer lack of an individual training schedule. In talking with leadership from other bases, we have come to understand that this problem that reserve training programs face is ubiquitous. EZTrain aims to increase the efficiency of reserve training time and ensure that trainees are able to complete tasks on-time for deployment readiness requirements.

Intuitive Dashboard

By providing leaders with an intuitive digital dashboard, they can view the statuses of everyone in their group at a glance. The dashboard is updated as trainees complete tasks and send confirmation texts on their phone which ensures that access to a computer is not a bottleneck for a group’s efficiency. With a real-time task completion board, Section Chiefs will understand exactly who is working and how much they are achieving. EZTrain also provides a messaging service that can be used by leadership and trainees to inform everyone in real time.

Smart Scheduling

The scheduling feature enables leadership to create smart schedules that ensure the maximum number of trainees are being trained on the most important tasks with the least amount of scheduling conflicts. Trainees will be able to see their schedules before their UTA weekend and plan accordingly. Since EZTrain’s internal database will keep track of finished tasks, leadership will be able to automatically generate updated trainee schedules for the next UTA weekend.

Complex Spreadsheet Synthesis

The Air Force reserve training program relies on many spreadsheets and databases to keep track of which tasks trainees have completed. EzTrain provides automatic master spreadsheet integration that creates a global log of all trainee datapoints with minimal user input.
